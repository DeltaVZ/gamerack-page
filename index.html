Your Library

Every game you own, at a glance.

Browse your collection as a cover grid or a list. Filter by play status — Playing, Finished, Skipped, or To Play — and sort however suits you. A live completion bar lets you keep an eye on your backlog.

  • Grid and list views
  • Play status badges
  • Backlog completion tracker
  • Works offline
The Rack

Track every copy you own.

Log physical discs and digital purchases separately — including steelbooks, press kits, limited runs, and collector sets. Record the store, platform, edition type, and quantity for each copy.

  • Physical and digital copies
  • Standard, Deluxe, and Collector's editions
  • Store tracking (Steam, PSN, GOG…)
  • Multiple quantities per copy
Playthroughs

Log how — and how long — you played.

Record individual playthroughs per game and platform. Set start and finish dates, hours played, and achievement status. Track DLC and mod runs separately, then filter your entire history any way you like.

  • Per-platform playthrough logs
  • Start date, finish date, hours played
  • Achievement completion status
  • DLC and mod run support
Game Catalog

Search thousands of games and add them in one tap.

Powered by IGDB, GameRack gives you access to a huge game database. Tap any result to see its full profile — release date, platforms, genres, summary, screenshots, trailers — then add it to your library without typing a single thing.

  • Powered by IGDB
  • Cover art and metadata filled automatically
  • Platforms, genres, developers, publishers
  • Screenshots and trailers
